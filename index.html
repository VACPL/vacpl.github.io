About VACPL

VACPL (Venkateshwara Automation & Control Pvt. Ltd.) is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra. Since 2001, we have been delivering best-in-class Electrical Panel Manufacturing and Automation Solutions with a focus on quality, precision, and innovation.

Our Core Services

Panel Manufacturing

Custom-built electrical control panels up to 6000A with 50kA/70kA fault levels.

Automation Engineering

PLC, SCADA, HMI, and IoT-based automation systems with advanced monitoring.

Commissioning

End-to-end setup, testing, and deployment of control systems at client sites.

Integration & IoT

Seamless connectivity between automation, IT, and data analytics platforms.
